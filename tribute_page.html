A Peak Into his Life...

  • 1981- Born at Ranchi, Bihar, India.
  • 1998- Steps into the arena of Proffesional Cricket.
  • 1999- Gets selected for State Level Cricket.
  • 2003- Plays for India-A against Zimbabwe, Kenya and Pakistan-A.
  • 2004- Debuts for India.
  • 2005- Scores 183*, the Highest Score by a Wicket-Keeper till date.

  • 2011- Guides India to lift their 2nd ICC Cricket World Cup Trophy. Wins 2nd IPL Trophy.
  • 2013- Guides India to lift their 2nd ICC Cricket Champions Trophy Cup at England.
  • 2014- Retires from Test Cricket.
  • 2016- Voluntarily steps down as India's Captain.
  • 2018- Becomes recipent of Padma Bhushan, India's third-highest civilian award. Wins 3rd IPL Trophy.
  • 2019- Completes a two-week stint with the Territorial Army in Jammu and Kashmir as Lieutenant-colonel.
